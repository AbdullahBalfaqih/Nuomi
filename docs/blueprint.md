# **App Name**: Interias Vision

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Visually appealing hero section to capture the user's attention and introduce the interior design studio.
- About Us Section: Detailed information about the studio's history, mission, and design philosophy.
- Featured Projects Gallery: Showcase of high-quality images of exclusive interior design projects with descriptions and tags.
- Services Overview: Clear presentation of offered services with descriptions and project counts.
- Client Testimonials: Carousel of client testimonials to build trust and credibility.
- Blog Section: Grid of blog posts providing design ideas, insights, and innovations.
- AI-Powered Interior Style Quiz: A tool using AI that analyzes user preferences and recommends interior design styles tailored to them.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Desaturated beige (#E0D8C4) to create a calm and sophisticated atmosphere.
- Background: Light off-white (#F5F5F5) to provide a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent: Warm gray (#808080) for subtle highlights and interactive elements.
- Body text: 'Inter', a versatile sans-serif, for a modern, clean reading experience.
- Headline text: 'Playfair', an elegant serif for impactful section titles, paired with 'PT Sans' for body text.
- Minimalist line icons in warm gray to complement the overall aesthetic.
- Clean and spacious layouts with fluid grids to ensure responsiveness across devices.
- Subtle fade-in and slide-up animations on scroll to enhance user experience.
